About Me

Sashay Chante is one of New Zealand’s professional Drag Queens!
Chante has been entertaining people in drag for the past 5 years! Sashaying all over the place she’s known for creating high energy acts and her wicked sense of humour.
Over her career she’s worked done; Public Events, Hosting, Special events, Cooperative functions, appearances and can guarantee to make sure everyone will be talking about and remember your event for a long time!

Sashay has Sass, Class and a big ol’ ass you can’t miss! With a charming smile, Fabulous Costumes! She is the perfect Drag Queen for your next event!

Hello Darling!
Ever since I was a baby I’ve been dressing in skirts, boots, dresses and all that gay stuff! I would even wear rainbow tights under my school uniform. Drag is one of the most magical things!
